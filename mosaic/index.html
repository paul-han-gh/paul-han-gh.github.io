The Problem We’re Solving

Many tutors and tutoring centers often use generic worksheets and one-size-fits-all lessons. They may help your child survive a test, but rarely help them learn how to learn. Mosaic changes that by identifying your child’s unique learning profile, academic gaps, and motivations in order to craft a personalized path forward.

How the Mosaic Assessment Works

Personality Assessment

We take an inside-out approach to understanding the way your child learns. Identifying their motivations and natural inclinations is central to our diagnostic.

Adaptive Proficiency Determination

A dynamic testing framework using machine learning for qualitative analysis on the way your child works through problems.

Introspective Dialogue

During this in-person session, we will do an informal interview to get to know your child from their own point-of-view. This low-stakes conversation helps us understand their perspective, communication skills, emotional intelligence, and engagement.

A Real Story

“I realized I wasn’t bad at math—I just needed to learn differently.”

When I entered 9th grade, math started to feel very hard. Traditional tutoring helped me get through assignments, but it didn’t help me understand the material. My confidence plummeted, and I started to believe I just wasn’t good at math.

What I didn’t know then was that I simply needed to learn at a pace and in a way that made sense for me. Because math builds on itself, that gap in confidence and mastery made future classes even harder.

Over time, I re-discovered my love for math when it was taught to me in a way that resonated, and with the help of mentors and teachers, I rebuilt my confidence in learning. I realized that the problem wasn’t my brain, but I was being taught in a way that didn’t resonate with the unique way I think. I was fortunate enough to have mentors that identified my unique wiring and treated it as an asset to be cherished.

Now, over a decade later, we are on a mission to help high school students experience that same breakthrough—to feel competent, confident, and capable of seeing their own potential.

~ Kimia Sadeghi, Founder of Mosaic

Frequently Asked Questions

How is it different from other assessments?

Mosaic takes a holistic approach that combines pedagogical insights across personality research, learning styles, and adaptive testing frameworks. Most assessments tend to focus on specifically personality tests or academics, making Mosaic a uniquely insightful assessment. Additionally, many tests have overly complicated reports, thus making it less actionable. If our assessment is not insightful or actionable, we will refund you in full, making it a no-risk commitment!

What will the in-person assessment be like?

We will ask your child questions about themselves to uncover their motivations, frustrations, goals, and learning preferences. We will also conduct a “Measures of Academic Performance” style math test that will be personalized to your student to give us an understanding of which concepts they have mastered, and which ones have room to grow.

Do we need to bring anything to the meeting?

No, we will provide all materials that may be used during the meeting.

Do you diagnose learning disabilities or other conditions?

No. We are not trained professionals in the field of psychology and we will never give you a “diagnosis” of your child.

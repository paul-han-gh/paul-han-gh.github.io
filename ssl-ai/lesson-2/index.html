Discover Multimodal AI

Welcome! "Multimodal AI" simply means artificial intelligence that can understand more than just text—it can look at pictures, listen to audio, and even watch videos.

This guide will walk you through a few friendly websites where you can try these tools yourself. Go at your own pace!

Not Started

1. Suno AI: Turn Your Voice into a Full Song

What it does: Suno AI is an incredibly powerful music generator. Using its "Audio Input" feature, you can record a simple voice memo of yourself humming, singing, or rapping. The AI will then build a complete, professional-sounding backing track around your voice in almost any genre you can imagine.

Listen to the examples below: I recorded a simple audio clip on my phone and had Suno AI transform it into three completely different musical styles. Have a listen to the original, and then check out the AI creations!

1. Original Raw Recording

A simple phone recording of me singing and rapping. No backing music.

2. Worship Rock Rendition

The same vocals, completely transformed into a full Worship Rock anthem.

3. Lyrical Hip Hop Rendition

Turned into a beat-driven, Lyrical Hip Hop track with professional production.

4. Melodic Dubstep Rendition

Reimagined with heavy electronic bass drops and atmospheric synths.

How to try it yourself:

  • Click the blue button below to visit Suno.com and create a free account.
  • Look for the "Upload Audio" option in the Create menu.
  • Upload a short recording of your voice (or record it right there on the site).
  • Type in the musical style you want (like "jazz piano" or "country acoustic") and hit create!

2. Adobe Firefly: Paint with Words

What it does: Adobe Firefly is an image generation tool. Instead of drawing with a mouse or pen, you simply type a description of what you want to see. The AI reads your words and "paints" a brand new picture for you in seconds.

Look at the example below: Here is what the AI created based purely on my text!

The Text Prompt I Used: "Give me a promotional product placement for Milk within a Polynesian tropical aesthetic"

How to try it yourself:

  • Click the blue button below to visit the Adobe Firefly website.
  • Look for the "Text to Image" feature.
  • Type a description of anything you can imagine into the search bar at the bottom.
  • Click "Generate" and watch four different versions of your idea appear!

3. Luma Dream Machine: Bring Scenes to Life

What it does: Luma Dream Machine takes things a step further by generating video. You can type a description of a moving scene, or even upload a still photograph, and the AI will animate it into a realistic 5-second video clip.

Watch the example below: I gave the AI a specific set of instructions, and it generated this short video entirely from scratch.

The Text Prompt I Used: "Make me a motivational video for people of all generations to be excited about the New Age of AI"

How to try it yourself:

  • Click the blue button below to visit Luma AI.
  • Find the text bar where you can enter a prompt.
  • Describe an action, a camera movement, or a scene you want to see moving.
  • It may take a minute or two to "render" (create) the video, so be patient!

4. Hugging Face Spaces: The AI Playground

What it does: Hugging Face is like a giant library where scientists and programmers share their AI creations with the public. Their "Spaces" section is a collection of thousands of mini AI applications. You can find everything from tools that colorize old black-and-white photos to AI that can chat with you like a historical figure.

A Quick Note on Exploring: Because these tools are made and shared by people all over the world, you might occasionally see the letters "NSFW" in an app's title. This stands for "Not Safe For Work" and means the content is explicit. We recommend simply skipping past those and clicking on family-friendly sounding tools!

How to try it yourself:

  • Click the link below to go to the Spaces homepage.
  • Scroll through the "Trending" list to see what's popular right now.
  • Click on anything that looks interesting to you and play around! Each "Space" has its own unique instructions.

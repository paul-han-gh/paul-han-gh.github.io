Concept

S.M.A.R.T.
Goals

Specific: Clear and well-defined, eliminating ambiguity.

Measurable: Includes concrete criteria for tracking progress.

Achievable: Realistic and attainable given current resources.

Relevant: Matters to your broader objectives and aligns with your overall vision.

Time-bound: Anchored to a strict deadline to create urgency.
